Our Process

1. Getting to know your shop

Unlike many web site solutions for Auto Repair shops, our sites are focused on your business, and tailored to match the unique aspects of your shop. That means the first step in our process toward increasing your car count is getting you know you,your shop, and your customers so that we can provide an incredibly tailored site that will fit your business like a finely tailored suit, and look just as good.

2. Writing Your Content

Once we get enough information about you and your business, our copy writers get to work by creating new content that best represents you, and helps you to stand out from your competition. Since this content is written to best represent you, and to speak directly to your customers, this produces high quality content that search engines will love just as as your customers do.

3. Picking Your Images

With content written, our design experts look through our library of high-resolution/ high quality images, and select the perfect images to match your color scheme, your business identity, and the content that we have crafted for you to bring content, design, and imagery together for a powerful online presence that represents your business at its best.

4. Building Your Site

Now that we have all the parts, and know precisely where we are going, our site builders jump right in, weaving the content, images, and your branding together to produce a website that is fast for your users, represents your unique business, and makes sure the search engines know who you are. We use the latest web technologies, and pick a stack and underlying technologies that best match your needs and budget. Whether we build your site ShopPro, ShopPro Plus, or a completely custom platform, you get a fast and secure site that will win.

5. Launching It!

Once we have your site just right, and you have given us the go ahead, we move into launching your site. We partner with some of the strongest web hosting companies out there, regardless of the technology used to build your site, in order to ensure that we are able to provide you with top-quality web hosting. Our chosen partners ensure that we are able to deliver a site that will always be up, and always be performant.

6. Continuous Improvement

Unlike many specialized web hosting platforms, launching your site is not the end of the journey. We will keep an eye on current technologies and best practices on the web, and we will continue optimizing your site, improving the design and usability while improving and optimizing the copy for your prospective customers and search engines alike.
